All time favourite food

Poutine

Poutine is a dish originating from the Canadian province of Quebec consisting of
French fries and cheese curds topped with a brown gravy.

Cheesecake

Cheesecake is a sweet dessert consisting of one or more layers. The main, and thickest layer,
consists of a mixture of soft, fresh cheese, eggs, and sugar; if there is a bottom layer
it often consists of biscuits or a graham cracker crust baked to perfection.

Ice Cream

Ice cream is a sweetened frozen food typically eaten as a snack or dessert. It is usually made
from dairy products, such as milk and cream, and often combined with fruits or other
ingredients and flavors.

Conclusion

On an individual level, we grow up eating the food of our cultures. Food is an important part
of culture. Traditional cuisine is passed down from one generation to the next. It also operates
as an expression of cultural identity. There are a variety of foods around the world that
each have their own unique flavour and style. So go out and try something new!
